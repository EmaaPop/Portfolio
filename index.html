Also into wellness and a certified 200h Yoga Teacher.





My top 3 projects:

  • Data Science: Air Pollution and Happiness: a geopolitical issue?

    • I was part of an interdisciplinary team that created data visualisations for the correlation between happiness levels and pollution levels in England over a 10-year period. Although I oficcialy had the role of a Web Designer, we all participated to the data collection, cleaning, translation and visualisation,summary statistics and correlation matrices.

  • UX/UI and App Development: Breathworld

    • I created the whole user interface and user journey of the app and and have participated at user research, impact analysis and competition research.

  • Art Show: Art Event at W1 Curates part of Last Night Collective

    • Featured my art at Volume I of LNC and helped organise the event by communicating with the office manager and the local technical team. Lead the creative curation and helped future artists prepare for showcasing their art on the screens. Through curated events, Last Night Collective will provide rising artists with visibility and offer friends, new and old, a safe space to connect, network, share ideas, and create. The result? Immense social capital. As we consume more marketing and advertising than ever before, no longer can companies afford to build brands first and keep community as an afterthought. Community must come first. The Last Night Collective draws attendees from the worlds of art, music, finance, tech, curation, production and film. By way of a trusted network, this series is poised to become a watering hole for the cutting-edge, offering a springboard for collaboration, creativity, collection, and connection.


Work in progress:

  • An IoT system that generates a heatmap of a room. I am using the Arduino MKR WiFi 1010 board to create a connected system of sensors and atcuators that collect data in the cloud and use that to generate a 3D map of temperatures in a room. This uses MQTT messaging and C programming for Arduino.
  • An interactive app where users can use their breathing rate as a control to create Augmented Reality visualisations. I want to explore how biometrics can be used to create 3D worlds and communities. This is a continuation of the Breathworld App.
