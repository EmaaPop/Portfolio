Art Event at W1 Curates

Through curated events, Last Night Collective will provide rising artists with visibility and offer friends, new and old, a safe space to connect, network, share ideas, and create. The result? Immense social capital. As we consume more marketing and advertising than ever before, no longer can companies afford to build brands first and keep community as an afterthought. Community must come first. The Last Night Collective draws attendees from the worlds of art, music, finance, tech, curation, production and film. By way of a trusted network, this series is poised to become a watering hole for the cutting-edge, offering a springboard for collaboration, creativity, collection, and connection.
