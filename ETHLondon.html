App


In 2 days we built an app that has an NFT Marketplace built on Tezos with smart contracts written in Pythonand AI chatbots available as NFTs. We built a decentralized social media network on Tezos where:

  1. We fix a broken $207bn a year social media ad model with a decentralized targeted ad model (inspired by Brave). We are connecting offchain Google Analytics data from the world’s largest advertisers with onchain 1UP social media consumer data.
  2. Users can purchase Mixed Reality and AI Celebrity Chatbot NFTs on the social network which are stored in the Tezos wallet and activate them in Google AR Coreand their social feed.
  3. Users receive 25% of the ad revenue they generate in Tezos microcrypto payments as they scroll social media.
  4. Users have control of the data used to target them with advertisements via a Privacy Portal hosted on Tezos.
  5. All bots, trolls and ad fraud are eliminated from social media with digitally ID verified account using Rarimo.
